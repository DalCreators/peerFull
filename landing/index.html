FAQ

Questions

Is CodeSync free to use?

Yes! The free tier gives you real-time code sync, colored cursors, and text chat for 2 people with 30-minute sessions. Upgrade to Pro for video/audio calling and unlimited time.

How does the video calling work?

Video is peer-to-peer using WebRTC — your video stream goes directly to your teammate's browser, not through our servers. This means lower latency and better privacy.

What happens when the 30-minute session ends?

The extension disconnects you with a warning. You can immediately start a new free session. Pro users have unlimited time.

Can I use this for a class assignment or hackathon?

Absolutely — that's exactly who CodeSync is for. Just make sure your course allows collaboration tools (most pair programming assignments do).

How do I activate my Pro license?

After payment, you'll receive a license key like CODESYNC-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX by email. In VS Code, open the command palette (Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+P) and run "CodeSync: Activate License Key".

Is my code sent to your servers?

Document updates are relayed through the signaling server as encrypted Yjs deltas — we never store your source code. Video/audio is peer-to-peer and never touches our servers.
